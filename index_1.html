Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I lose my phone?

+

Your notes are safe on-chain. Just connect your wallet on a new device and everything syncs back. You'll need to reset reminders, but your content is permanent.

How does the AI work?

+

The AI runs locally on your device, scanning your text for time-based content like "tomorrow at 3PM" or "next Friday". It's simple, private, and doesn't send your data anywhere.
